\Application\EntityAnonymousJudge

Entity representing a category of anonymous/generic judge.

Events typically have a related Judge entity. A Judge is a Person with attributes like last name, firstname. But some events have a "generic" judge, e.g., the Magistrate who happens to be on duty when the event takes place. There are also occasionally "not applicable" cases, such as when some agency or department requests a document translation for general use, rather than on behalf of a particular judge.
